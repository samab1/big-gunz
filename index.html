BIG GUNZ 💪

3 days a week · alternate A and B · e.g. Mon / Wed / Fri

🧠 Coach's rules

  • Warm up first: 2–3 min — arm circles, 10 bodyweight squats, a few light halos, short dead hang.
  • Go heavier when it's easy: if you finish every set with good form and 2+ reps still "in the tank", move up in weight next session. That's how you get bigger.
  • Rest between sets: ~90 sec on the big moves (squat, press, deadlift, row), ~60 sec on curls, flys, shrugs, halos.
  • Form beats weight. If form breaks, the set is over. Watch the video again whenever unsure.
  • Eat & sleep: protein with every meal and 7–8 h sleep — that's where the muscle is actually built.
  • Never two days in a row. Rest days are part of the program.
